# Product Principles

- Build an internal tool, not a demo.
- Default to clarity over novelty.
- Tables matter more than decorative cards.
- Make score explanations obvious.
- Make data lineage visible.
- Keep AI in the backend and out of the product personality.
- Every screen should help a rep decide who to call next.
- Honest stubs are better than fake integrations.
- Keep the app shippable at every stage.
